    WIRECARE brings a safer and smarter lifestyle for your family

    #electrical fire#outlet safety#receptacle outlet check
    2021/12/03


    Do you know the electrical fire is one of the reasons causing the house on fire? Ignoring the dust on the outlets or not checking the plugs and the wiring system on a regular basis could easily cause fire. Introducing wirecare from brook livin, an easy-to-use tool to check receptacle outlets and extension cords in your house.


    Simply plug-in wirecare to every receptacle outlet in your house. Within 10 seconds, wirecare lighting system gives you the status of all your receptacle outlets; green light means good and safe, orange light needs more attention and uses with caution, and red light means danger, immediate replacement is highly recommended.


    Through wirecare, we provide a safe and worry-free life to your family.
